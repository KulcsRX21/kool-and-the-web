# Big match against Real Madrid in the Champions League

Published on: 2026-04-15

Welcome to my _Bayern blog_ about loving FC Bayern! Here, I will share my thoughts about them.

## What Bayern have accomplished

1. **Breaking Records**: On the weekend, they have scored their 102th (and then some more) goals in this league season which is the the new record in the Bundesliga.

2. **Playing as a Team**: Every player plays for the common goal and there are no individuals who do not fit in the team.

3. **Trusting the Manager**: Kompany is loved by every fan and the players trust him too! It was a long time ago when a manager got this amount of support!
